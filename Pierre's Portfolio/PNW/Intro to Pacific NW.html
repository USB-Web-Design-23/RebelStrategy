BRIEF INTRODUCTION


There are no official border lines that declare where the Pacific Northwest (PNW) exactly is. PNW is approximately at the northwest corner of the United States and the Southwest corner of Canada. Large cities, such as Seattle, Portland, and Vancouver, are top options to go as you travel to PNW.
