Hip Hop and Rap

The beginning

Hip Hop music began in New York City in the early 1970s and can be composed of several common elements. It is usally built around a central rythym which is built around the drums of the track. Many tracks also having rapping which is a rhyming and rythmatic chant. In these days it was commonly associated by Dj'ing and graffiti art. Also lots of hip hop started with the use of samples or taking pieces from other songs and creating a beat around it.






Old school

Old school hip is commonly characterized in the 1980s. The first widely recognized Hip Hop record was Sugarhill's gang's, Rappers delight. Throughout this time hip hop spread like wildfire across the country and into other countries aswell. Large hubs started to pop up in Philidelphia, Chicago and even seattle. The genre also started taking off in Puerto Rico too. Many experts say that disco music had a very large impact on Hip Hop because djing and the music being catered to danvers.






The Golden Age

The golden Age of Hip Hop was considered from 1986 to 1997. This brought about household known artists such as Public Enemy, A tribe called Qeust and De la soul. This is a time when people say the genre was reimagined. The artists of the time took was before and added and perfected it. This also brought along several subgenre's such as "gansta rap" and "East coast".
