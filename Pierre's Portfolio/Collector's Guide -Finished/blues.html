Blues Music


Introduction

In 1960s, Blues music originated from the Deep South of the United States. "Blues" is generally thought to have a "sad" mood. The origin of Blues music is affected by several distinct musical genres, such as chants, spirituals, work songs, ring shouts. The Blues music is self, also influenced jazz and originated further styles of Blues music, such as R&B.





Formats

Stanza: Blues music usually use "AAB" structure, which, two Stollen stanza (Aufgesangs) following a different stanza called Abgesang.

Chord: A common chord progression of Blues music is shown below. -> "I" means tonic (root), "V" means dominant, "IV" means subdominant
