Rock N' Roll


Introduction

Rock music originates in the United States in the late 1940s and early 1950s, expand to various sub-styles in mid 1960s. Rock music is influenced by jazz, blues (especially R&B), African American music and country music.

Rock music plays a crucial rule in carrying culture and ideas. For instance, rock music sometimes serves as a media of anti-war, you can find anti-war elements in many rock songs, such as "Hammer to Fall" by Queen, "21 Guns" by Green Day.





Patterns

Rock music usually uses a "4/4 time signiture" and a "verse-chorus" form.

This pattern usually give rock music a "progression," which, emotions of author can be gradually magnified as the song goes on.
